Income By Major, Region, and College Type

By Hassan Koroma

Big Idea

Wondering if that Philosophy major will really help you pay the bills? Think you're set with an Engineering degree? Choosing a college major is a complex decision evaluating personal interest, difficulty, and career prospects. Consequently, the discussion about potential earnings following graduation is very common among college students. Like me, fellow students also weigh the cost-benefit ratio of their major after graduation, especially with the loans we accumulate.

Therefore, I’d like to explore the variations in salaries of different college degrees across the United States. I’m mainly interested in visualizing the majors/fields that give the greatest or least return in investment, but it’s also fascinating to see potential jobs that are related to one’s major and how salaries change as one moves further in their specialized field of study.

Whether you're in school or navigating the postgrad world, join me as we explore the short and long term financial implications of this major decision.
